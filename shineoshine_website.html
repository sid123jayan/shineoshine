Our Story

A Legacy Born ofDevotion & Craft

Shine O Shine began in Chennai as a labour of love — breathing new life into old brass and bronze wares. From humble beginnings in Valasaravakkam, we have grown into Chennai's most trusted source for authentic pooja items, ethnic home décor, and sacred idols.

We specialise in intricately carved brass sculptures, traditional oil lamps, and revered panchaloha (five-metal) idols — each piece polished with the same devotion it was made with. Our clientele spans celebrities, five-star hotels, and devout homes alike.

Vineetha Nambiar Founder & Master Artisan
